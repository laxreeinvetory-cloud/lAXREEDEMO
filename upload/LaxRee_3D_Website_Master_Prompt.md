## DESIGN THESIS (read this before building)

**Subject:** LaxRee is not a generic "hotel supplies store" — it's an 11-year-old OEM *manufacturer* (own factory lines for Minibar and Safe Locker) and Ajmer's largest hospitality exhibition centre, serving 1-5★ hotel procurement teams pan-India with 700+ SKUs across Amenities, Furniture, Linen, Roofing and Dome.

**Concept — "The Corridor at Dusk":** the page is structured like walking down a lit hotel corridor at night. Sections alternate between warm charcoal ("closed door") and warm ivory ("open door") — each full-bleed section is a room with its own lighting, and a single brass accent glows consistently through every room, tying the walk together. This is a deliberate structural device (not decoration) — it mirrors your real headline, "Opening Doors To A Whole New World Of Hotel Supplies."

**Signature element:** ONE real 3D object — your own-manufactured flagship product (Minibar or Safe Locker) — rotates slowly in the hero under a soft brass rim-light. This is the single "wow" moment of the site. Every other section stays quiet and disciplined around it: glass cards, restrained tilt/parallax, no competing gimmicks. (Per current 2026 web design direction, product-accurate 3D — not decorative 3D — is what actually reduces B2B purchase hesitation and reads as premium rather than templated.)

**Explicitly avoid:** generic AI-site defaults — no cream-background+terracotta-accent look, no black-background+neon-green look, no numbered "01/02/03" markers on things that aren't a real sequence (your five categories are not steps in a process, so don't badge them like one).

### Design Tokens
| Token | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `--charcoal` | `#12100D` | dark section backgrounds |
| `--ivory` | `#F7F3EA` | light section backgrounds |
| `--brass` | `#C6A15B` | primary accent, CTAs, the recurring "glow" |
| `--brass-light` | `#E4C989` | hover state on brass elements |
| `--emerald` | `#1E4638` | secondary accent — trust/certification band only |
| `--ink` | `#1A1712` | text on ivory |
| `--sand` | `#B7AC97` | muted text / hairline borders on charcoal |
| `--ink-muted` | `#6B6455` | muted text on ivory |

**Type system:** Display = **Fraunces** (variable, optical size + soft-weight axis; use 500–600 weight, never bold-blocky) for all headlines — warm, editorial, not the generic Playfair look. Body = **Work Sans** (400/500) for paragraphs and UI copy. Data/labels = **IBM Plex Mono** (500, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08em) for every stat, eyebrow label, certification code and price-adjacent number — a deliberate nod to the technical/spec-sheet nature of B2B procurement. Import all three from Google Fonts.

**Radius & glass:** cards `24px` radius, pills/buttons `999px`. Glass-on-charcoal = `background: rgba(255,255,255,0.04); backdrop-blur: 20px; border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`. Glass-on-ivory = `background: rgba(255,255,255,0.55); backdrop-blur: 16px; border: 1px solid rgba(26,23,18,0.06)`.

## MIGRATION NOTE — read before you spend dev time on this

Your current site is WordPress + Elementor + WP Rocket, running a 700+ SKU catalogue and an active SEO blog ("Hospitality Trends"). This prompt describes a **from-scratch React rebuild**, which is the right call if you want the full 2026 3D/motion experience above — but it also means re-platforming your catalogue, blog, and dealer/lead forms, not just re-skinning them. Two honest paths, so you can choose deliberately instead of by default:

- **Path A — Full rebuild (what this prompt assumes):** React/Next.js frontend, either with content hard-coded initially or, better, using WordPress purely as a **headless CMS** (REST/GraphQL) so your existing 700+ products and blog posts don't need manual re-entry. Higher effort, but gives you the complete design above, better long-term performance, and full control over the 3D/motion layer.
- **Path B — Lower-risk add-on:** keep WordPress/Elementor as-is and add a 3D/AR layer with a plugin (e.g. PausAR for Elementor) just to the hero and product pages, plus Elementor's own scroll/hover animation tools for the bento and card sections. You lose some of the bespoke feel above, but ship in days instead of weeks and keep your SEO/blog/catalogue exactly as they are.

Given you asked specifically for a 3D, trend-forward *rebuild*, this document targets Path A. If timeline or budget is tight, it's worth deciding explicitly rather than defaulting into a half-finished rebuild.
